About us

Greetings from AtlasFly, where flight and innovation meet! We are a dynamic team, Faiz Ansari, Vaibhav Raut, Shree Shinde, and Jay Patel, and we are passionate about transforming the travel industry.

Using machine learning, we set out to make precise predictions about flight costs. Come along with us as we transform the way you travel by making every trip with AtlasFly an exciting new experience. Buckle up for a new era in airline ticket predictions!

About Project

Our project, Flight F are Prediction, combines machine learning and web development. We build a user-friendly interface with JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap integrated together.

In the meantime, Python-powered machine learning improves the prediction accuracy of airline fares. It's a tech hybrid designed to make your travel planning easier.

Objective

Our goal with the Flight Fare Prediction project is to transform travel planning. By utilizing HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and Python-driven machine learning, we hope to provide a platform that works seamlessly together.

Our mission is to empower users to make informed and efficient travel decisions by offering accurate and automatic flight fare predictions.

Learnings

In the learning phase of our Flight Fare Prediction venture, our team dives deep into mastering HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and Python-based machine learning.

Through hands-on experience, we aim to enhance our skills in web development and predictive modeling. This journey fuels our commitment to staying at the forefront of technology and refining our expertise for future innovations.
